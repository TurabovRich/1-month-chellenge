Dumpling

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound cooked, chopped turkey meat
  2. 3 cups water
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  5. 1 (12 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough 
            Place the turkey, water, salt and pepper in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.
             Reduce heat and simmer 30 to 40 minutes, or until a broth has formed.
        
            Spread flour on a medium cutting board or other flat surface. Roll out biscuit dough 
            and cut it into 1x2 inch pieces. Drop the pieces into the broth and cook over low heat
             approximately 15 minutes.
